Trapezoid

Trapezoid (US), Trapezium (UK)

A flat shape with four sides

Has at least one pair of parallel sides

 

 

Bases, legs, height

Parallel sides are called bases

Other sides are called legs or lateral sides

Distance from base to base is known as height or altitude

Area

The area is the average of two bases times the height

1/2 x (b1 + b2) x h

Above trapezoid has an area of

1/2 x (6+2) x 4 = 16 square unit

Perimeter

The perimeter is sum of all sides (bases and legs)

b1 + b2 + l1 + l2

Above trapezoid has a perimeter of

6 + 3 + 4 + 5 = 18 unit of length
