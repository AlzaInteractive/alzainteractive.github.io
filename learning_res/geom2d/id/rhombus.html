Rhombus

Rhombus has four equal sides.

Each opposite sides are parallel to each other

Each opposite angles are the same size

 

Perimeter

Perimeter of a rhombus is equals to

four times of its side length

Above example has a perimeter of  4 X s

Area

Area of a rhombus is equals to

1/2 x diagonal1 x diagonal2

Above example has an area of  1/2 d1 X d2

Reflectional Symmetry

Rhombus has two axes of line (reflectional) symmetry

Rotational Symmetry

Rhombus has radial (rotational) symmetry of order 2

Diagonals

Its diagonals cross each other at right angles

Each one bisects/cuts rhombus equally in half
