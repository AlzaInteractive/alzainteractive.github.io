Square

Square is a flat shape with four equal sides.

Each corner is a right angle (90o)

Each opposite sides of a square are parallel to each other

Perimeter

Perimeter of a square is equals to

four times of its side length

Reflectional Symmetry

A square has four axes of line (reflectional) symmetry

Rotational Symmetry

A square has radial (rotational) symmetry of order 4

Circle

 

A circle is a simple close shape
