Parallelogram

Parallelogram has four equal sides

Opposite sides are parallel to each other

Opposite sides are also equal in lengths

AB = DC and AD = BC

Angles

Opposite angles are equals

Adjacent angles are supplementary, means both add up to 180o

α + β = 180o

Parallelogram where all angles are right (90o) is a rectangle

Area

The area of a parallelogram is equals to :

base x height

Above parallelogram has an area of 5 x 4 = 20 square unit

Perimeter

The area of a parallelogram is equals to

2 x (base + side length)

Diagonals

The diagonals bisect each other

They intersect each other exactly at their midpoint

Diagonals

Diagonals of a rectangle is equals to

√width2 + height2

(phytagoras formula)

Above rectangle has a diagonals of  √42 + 32
