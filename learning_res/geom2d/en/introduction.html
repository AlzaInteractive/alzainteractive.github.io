Introduction

A plane is two dimensional (2D), a flat shaped surface

Square

Square is a flat shape with four equal sides.

Each corner is a right angle (90o)

Each opposite sides of a square are parallel to each other

Rectangle

 

Rectangle is a flat shape with four sides

Each opposite sides has equal lengths

Each opposite sides is also parallel to each other

Like a square, each corner of a rectangle is also a right angle (90o)

Triangle

      

Triangles has three sides and three angles

Sum of each corner angles equals to (180o)

Parallelogram

  

Parallelogram has four sides

Opposite sides are parallel to each other

Opposite sides has the same length

Trapezoid

  

Trapezoid has four sides

A pair of the opposite sides are parallel to each other

Also known as Trapezium (UK)

Rhombus

 

Rhombus has four equal sides

Rhombus has a diamond like shape

Opposite sides are parallel to each other

Kite

 

Well, it is look like a kite, hence the name

Has two pairs of side

Each pair has two equal length adjacent sides

Circle

 

A circle is a simple close shape

Each point of a circle has the same distance from the center
