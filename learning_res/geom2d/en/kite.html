Kite

Kite has four sides.

It has two pairs of sides

Each pair is made of two adjacent sides with equal length

 

Perimeter

Kite with shorter side x, and longer side y, has a perimeter of:

x + x + y + y or 2(x + y)

Area

Area of a kite is equals to half of its first times its second diagonal

1/2 x diagonal1 x diagonal2

Above example has an area of  1/2 d1 X d2

Reflectional Symmetry

Kite has one axes of line (reflectional) symmetry

Rotational Symmetry

Kite has a radial (rotational) symmetry of order 1

Diagonals

Its diagonals cross each other at right angles

One of the diagonal bisects/cuts kite equally in half
