Circle

A circle is a round flat shape.

Its edge is made of a curved line.

All points on the line has equal distance from the center

 

Radius

Radius is the distance from the center to the edge of the circle

It is usually symbolized with the letter 'r'

Diameter

Diameter is a line that cut accross a circle thru it center

Diameter is twice the radius

It is usually symbolized with the letter 'd'

Circumference

The circumference of a circle is the perimeter

It is the distance around the edge of the circle

Formula for circle circumference are

π x d or 2 x π x r

where value of π in two decimal places is 3.14

Above example has a perimeter of 3.14 x 2 x 7 = 43.96

Area

Formula for the circle area are:

π x r2   or   1/4 x π x d2

Above example has a area of  3.14 x 7 x 7 = 153.86
