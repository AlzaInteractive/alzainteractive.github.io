Triangle

Triangle is a flat shape with three sides.

Triangle has three (tri) angles

      

Classification by sides length

Based on the length ratio of its three sides, a triangle can be either :

equilateral, iscosceles, or scalene

Equilateral Triangle

Equilateral triangle has all sides with equal length

Consequently, each angles is 180o/3 = 60o

It has rotational symmetry of order 3

It also has reflectional symmetry in 3 axes

Iscosceles Triangle

Equilateral triangle has two sides with equal length

It has order 1 rotational symmetry

Reflectional symmetry in 1 axes

Scalene Triangle

Scalene triangle has all 3 sides with unequal lengths

It has rotational symmetry of order 1

It has zero (no) axis of reflectional symmetry

Sum of all angles

For any given triangle, the sum off all angles is always add up to 180o

Classification by angles sizes

Based on the size of its three angles, a triangle can be either :

obtuse, acute, or right

Obtuse

An obtuse triangle has one angle with size larger than 90o

Acute

An acute triangle has all 3 angles with size less than 90o

An equilateral triangle is an example of acute triangle

Right

A right triangle has one angle with size exactly 90o
