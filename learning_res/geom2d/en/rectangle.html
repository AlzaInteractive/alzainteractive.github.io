Rectangle

Square is a flat shape with four sides.

Each corner is a right angle (90o)

Each opposite sides has the same length to each other

Each opposite sides are parallel to each other

Perimeter

Perimeter of a rectangle is equals to

two times the width + two times the height

OR

two times ( width + height )

Above rectangle has a perimeter of  2 X (5 + 3)

Area

Area of a rectangle is equals to

width x height

Above rectangle has an area of  5 X 3

Reflectional Symmetry

A square has two axes of line (reflectional) symmetry

Rotational Symmetry

A square has radial (rotational) symmetry of order 2

Diagonals

Diagonals of a rectangle is equals to

√width2 + height2

(phytagoras formula)

Above rectangle has a diagonals of  √42 + 32
